Users are allowed to connect their Facebook account with the Website, for the purpose of tracking users for the favourite confessions feature, and to be able to like and comment on confessions on the Website. All submitted confessions are meant to be anonymous. As such, we do not track or store any personal identifable information, like your Facebook account information if you have logged in. We do not track IP addresses to submitted confessions in the database.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. NUSWhispers uses cookies to help identify and track visitors, their usage of the Website, and their website access preferences. Visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using the Website, with the drawback that certain features of the Website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
